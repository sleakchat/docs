<Update label="23 Nov 2025" description="Knowledge Base">

### Knowledge Documents

You can now upload custom documents to give your agent granular context about your company.

- **Semantic Context:** Agent automatically searches your documents for relevant info when drafting
- **Unlimited Uploads:** Add as many documents as you need
- **Better Accuracy:** Drafts are now informed by your specific pricing, FAQs, and whatever context is important for your use case

[Learn more](/features/knowledge-base)

</Update>

<Update label="12 Nov 2025" description="Outlook Support">

### Microsoft Outlook Integration

The Untyped beta is now open for Microsoft Outlook in addition to Gmail.

- Full Outlook inbox sync
- Draft generation for Outlook emails
- Category-based email organization

</Update>
