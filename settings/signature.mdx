---
title: 'Add your email signature'
description: 'Copy your Outlook or Gmail signature to include it in Untyped draft replies'
---

<Note>
  Please note that some signatures may not display correctly due to complex formatting or included media. If your signature isn't looking as expected, [contact us](/support) so we can help you get it
  set up.
</Note>

<Steps>

<Step title='Copy your signature'>
  Copy your signature from a new email within Gmail/Outlook.
  <Warning>This does not work with plain text in existing mails - copy directly from the text field in the email editor or from your Email settings.</Warning>
</Step>

{/* prettier-ignore */}
<Step title='Navigate to email settings'>
  Open your dashboard and navigate to the signature section

    1. Open settings
    2. Click the 'mailboxes' dropdown
    3. Select the right mailbox
    4. Click 'Email settings'

</Step>

<Step title='Paste and activate'>
  Paste your signature in the designated field and save it. It will be added to all new Untyped drafts from now on.
  <Info>Make sure that the Email signature setting checkbox is enabled</Info>
</Step>

</Steps>
