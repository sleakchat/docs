---
title: 'Add your email signature'
description: 'Copy your Outlook or Gmail signature to include it in Untyped draft replies'
---

<Steps>

<Step title='Copy your signature'>Copy your signature from a new (or previously sent) email within Gmail/Outlook.</Step>

<Step title='Navigate to email settings'>Open your dashboard and navigate to the signature section. Open settings → navigate to 'mailboxes' → select the right mailbox → click 'email settings'.</Step>

<Step title='Paste and activate'>Paste your signature in the designated field and activate the setting.</Step>

</Steps>
